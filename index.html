PillPal - Medication reminder

A medicine reminder and tracker app whch helps you stay on schedule, track your recovery and never miss a medication slot.

Google Play App Store

App Features

The app offers personalized reminders for each day, as well as important drug interaction warnings, missed medication alerts, refill reminders when you're running low, and scheduling tools.

Fixed daily, weekly, monthly schedules

Easy to set up schedules

Measure heartrate

Keep track of heart rate and other parameters

Store prescriptions and other records

Save prescriptions for quick access

Missed medication Warnings

Strong notifications for missed medications

Progress Tracking

Generate Daily, weekly or monthly reports

User Profile

Create a profile and log your personal information

An all-in-one user friendly app

This app has been designed to be accessible and easy to use for all age groups keeping functionality and ease of use as the priority

  • Intuitive visual interface with semantic structural elements
  • Sharp and clear cues, noises & reminders
  • Registration and sign-up not required

Testimonials

We love feedback as we can constantly improve the app to make the experience optimal for the user. Here are some of our testimonials:

Syam Singh

Consultant

I'm really, really pleased with how this app works. I tend to be terrible with timekeeping thanks to a couple of mental health issues I have, but Message pops up on my screen as a full screen reminder that I have to take my meds. It interrupts what I'm doing so I have no choice but to take them! You can snooze the app, granted, but the pop up reminder somehow helps me to remember that actually, taking my meds is important and I really should take them on time.
